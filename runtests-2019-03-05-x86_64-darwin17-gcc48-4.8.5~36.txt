Synopsis  
--------  
See also gmp, mpc, mpfr, dragonegg34. Use NOJAVA=X to disable Java. Classes in Fortran are partially implemented, ie no finalizers. Other Fortran bugs include but not limited to PRs 47034, 44672, 54070, 57965, 64674, 64787, 64933, and 65428. Whereas, the unofficial backporting of Deferred Length Strings is problematic as well. The PR 48097 is Java related, Darwin Specific and always open. The PR 83531 has not been finalised yet.
